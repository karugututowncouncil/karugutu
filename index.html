Karugutu Town Council Mandate

Karugutu Town Council is the legally established governing authority for Karugutu Town. Created by Statutory Instrument No. 48 of 2017 under the Local Governments (Rating) Act, 2005, Karugutu Town Council operates as a lower local government within Ntoroko District.
In accordance with the Local Governments Act (Cap 243), it is mandated to manage urban areas on behalf of both the District and Central Governments. Karugutu Town Council’s core role is to decentralize service delivery, promote democratic participation in decision-making, and drive local economic development within its jurisdiction.
The Council provides essential public services such as primary education, primary health care, sanitation, waste management, and local transport infrastructure. It is also responsible for planning and implementing development programmes, including physical planning, land use regulation, and enforcement of building standards to ensure orderly urban growth.
In addition, the Town Council exercises legislative and administrative authority through the formulation of byelaws, the collection of local revenues, and immediate oversight of public service delivery points within its jurisdiction.

About Karugutu Town Council

Karugutu Town Council is located in Ntoroko District, Western Region of Uganda, covering an area of 87 km². Officially separated from Karugutu Sub-county in 2010 following the creation of Ntoroko District from Bundibugyo District, it attained autonomous urban status under Statutory Instrument No. 48 of 2017. The Council borders the Toro-Semliki National Park and serves as the strategic administrative hub.

Functions of the Mayor

The Mayor is the political head of the Town Council. He presides over all council meetings, performs ceremonial and civic functions, hosts local and foreign dignitaries, and leads the council in developing strategies for the Town Council's development. The Mayor monitors administration, provides guidance to ward administrations, and is answerable to the council.

Karugutu Town Council – Vision 2040
"A vibrant, inclusive, climate-resilient and well-governed town with sustainable economic growth and improved quality of life for all."

By 2040, Karugutu will be a thriving economic hub powered by local enterprise, modern agriculture, small industries, and innovation. Infrastructure—roads, energy, and communication—will enable seamless connectivity. An inclusive society where all have access to quality social services. A climate-resilient community embracing clean energy and sustainable land use. Good governance, transparent, accountable, and participatory leadership will be the norm.
